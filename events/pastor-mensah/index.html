40 Years of Ministry

Journey of Faith

1986

Called to Ministry

Answered the call to serve God at age 24, beginning his journey as a youth pastor.

1992

Ordained as Pastor

Officially ordained and appointed to lead the Tema Community Church.

2000

Church Expansion

Led the construction of a new church building, expanding congregation to over 500 members.

2010

District Overseer

Appointed District Overseer, overseeing 15 churches across the Greater Accra Region.

2026

Retirement

After 40 faithful years, Pastor Mensah transitions into a well-deserved retirement, leaving a lasting legacy.
