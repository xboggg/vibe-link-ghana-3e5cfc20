Blessings for Baby Kwame

"Obi nkyere abofra Nyame"

No one teaches a child about God - meaning children have a natural connection to the divine.

- Akan Proverb

"Abofra bunu a, na ote n'agya dan mu"

When a child is respectful, he dwells in his father's house - may he always be blessed with good character.

- Akan Proverb

"Dua baako gye mframa a, ebu"

One tree standing alone cannot withstand the wind - may he always have family and community.

- Akan Proverb
