## Week 3: Influencer Outreach

### Micro-Influencer Campaign
- [ ] List 20 micro-influencers (5K-20K followers)
  - Wedding photographers
  - Event planners
  - Lifestyle bloggers
  - Recently engaged people
- [ ] DM template: "Hi [name], love your content! We're VibeLink Event - we create stunning digital invitations. Would love to gift you our service for your next event in exchange for honest content. Interested?"
- [ ] Offer 5 free services in exchange for posts
- [ ] Track responses and follow up

### Content Collaboration
- [ ] Partner with photographer for styled shoot
- [ ] Collaborate with event planner for joint content
- [ ] Guest post on wedding blog

## Week 4: Paid Advertising

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- [ ] Install Meta Pixel on website
- [ ] Create Custom Audiences:
  - Website visitors
  - Instagram engagers
  - Video viewers
- [ ] Launch Test Campaign:
  - Budget: GHS 500
  - Objective: Traffic
  - Audience: Ghana, 22-40, interested in weddings
  - Creative: Best design reveal video

### TikTok Spark Ads
- [ ] Identify top-performing organic video
- [ ] Boost as Spark Ad (GHS 200 test)
- [ ] Target: Ghana, 18-35
