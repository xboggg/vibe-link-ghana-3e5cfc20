## Executive Summary

VibeLink Event is positioned to become Ghana's premier digital event stationery and invitation platform. This strategy focuses on:

1. **Viral Mechanics** - Built-in sharing features that make customers your marketers
2. **Social Proof** - Leveraging real events to create FOMO
3. **Community Building** - Creating a movement, not just a service
4. **Strategic Partnerships** - Aligning with complementary businesses
5. **Content Dominance** - Owning the conversation around event planning in Ghana

**Goal:** 1,000 paying customers and 50,000 social media followers within 6 months.

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

#### Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Awareness**
- Objective: Video views, reach
- Budget: 30% of ad spend
- Audience: Broad Ghana, 22-45, interests: weddings, events
- Creative: Viral-style videos, design reveals

**Campaign 2: Consideration**
- Objective: Traffic, engagement
- Budget: 30% of ad spend
- Audience: Website visitors, engaged users
- Creative: Carousel of portfolio, testimonials

**Campaign 3: Conversion**
- Objective: Leads, purchases
- Budget: 40% of ad spend
- Audience: Warm audiences, lookalikes
- Creative: Offer-focused, urgency

- Recently engaged

**Key Success Factors:**
1. Consistency - Post daily, engage daily
2. Quality - Every piece of content reflects the brand
3. Authenticity - Real customers, real events, real stories
4. Speed - Move fast, iterate quickly
5. Community - Build relationships, not just transactions

**Remember:** The best marketing is a delighted customer sharing their beautiful invitation with 100+ guests. Every design is a marketing opportunity.
